About

Challenge Quiz is a free game of questions and answers to test your general knowledge. Think you're cultivated? Prove it on Challenge Quiz!.
Attend general knowledge quiz of nomber of questions you define. Attention you only have a certain amount of time(caluculated base on nombre of question you choose) per quiz! The more you answer quickly and you get points.

All quizzes are varied, the questions belongs to a certain themes you can define too. Our database has over 4000 questions and we add 10q daily. You have very little chance of getting two times on the same question.
